Statistic Abbreviations

epk
enemy players killed
eao
enemy armies ogged
eck
enemy carriers killed
pck
potential carriers killed
ab
armies bombed
pld
planets destroyed (reduced to 0 armies)
plt
planets taken
rsb
resources bombed
tac
total armies carried (doesn't count ones beamed to starbases or onto safe planets)
nad
net armies delivered (total delivered to enemy planets with <=3 armies PLUS total delivered to friendly planets with <3 armies MINUS those beamed up from a friendly planet with <3 armies)
fpk
friendly players killed
fao
friendly armies ogged
fck
friendly carriers killed
cak
carried armies killed
def
deaths by enemy fire (includes planets)
dpf
deaths by planetary fire (includes stars and tractor kills)
